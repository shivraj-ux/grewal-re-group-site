Who We Are

## Advisors first, agents second.

We are Grewal RE Group. An Austin real estate advisory team that sits at the intersection of luxury, relocation, residential, and investment work, with deep coverage of West Austin and the central + east growth corridors.

We are advisors first, agents second. The job is to focus on what actually moves the outcome — pricing, leverage, risk, timeline, and net result — not just opening doors.

That orientation comes from a simple belief: relationships precede transactions. The client is the hero. We are the guide. Every recommendation is backed by data, every uncomfortable answer is given straight, and every relationship continues long after closing, because the question after “should I buy?” is “how do we manage it for the next ten years?”

It's the kind of advisor work you'd want from a friend who happens to be excellent at this. Calm. Informed. Human. Prepared.

The opposite of pressure, hype, or guesswork — and the opposite of a Zillow ad.
