Shivraj approves before any redirect/merge/push is applied. Deliver planning documents first, do not edit or redirect pages until he signs off.
**Why:** Live luxury real estate site; mistakes are public and hurt the brand.
**How to apply:** For consolidation/redirect/schema work, produce a paste-ready plan and stop. Wait for explicit go-ahead before touching `_redirects`, `sitemap.xml`, or any HTML.

Copy writing rules for any suggested copy: plain human language, short sentences. NO dashes as sentence connectors (no em/en dashes, no hyphen-as-connector). Never use: straightforward, genuinely, honestly, dive into, leverage, delve.
**Why:** Brand voice is "People First. Straight Answers. Strong Results."
**How to apply:** Applies to all body copy, titles, meta descriptions, and merged content suggestions.
