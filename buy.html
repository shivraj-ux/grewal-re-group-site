The Austin Buying Process

1. Pre-Approval & Budget Setting

Start with pre-approval from a lender to know your buying power. Use our affordability calculator to set a realistic budget based on down payment, income, and current rates.

Calculate Your Budget

2. Neighborhood Search & Property Viewing

Explore Austin neighborhoods that match your lifestyle and budget. We cover 25+ communities from Westlake Hills to East Austin to emerging areas like Davenport Ranch.

Browse Communities

3. Offer & Negotiation

We help you submit competitive offers with clear contingencies and timelines. Austin's market requires strategy, local data, and confidence in negotiation.

4. Inspection, Appraisal & Final Walkthrough

Work with trusted inspectors and coordinate with your lender's appraisal team. A final walkthrough 24 hours before closing catches any last-minute issues.

5. Closing

Sign documents, transfer funds, and get the keys. Typically 30-45 days from accepted offer to closing day.
