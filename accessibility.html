Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Our commitment

Grewal RE Group is committed to making grewalregroup.com usable by everyone, including people who use assistive technology such as screen readers, screen magnification, voice recognition software, or keyboard-only navigation. We intend for this Site to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

What we do

We work toward WCAG 2.1 AA conformance through practices including:

  • Semantic HTML headings, landmarks, and a "skip to main content" link on every page
  • Descriptive alt text on informational images and decorative images marked so screen readers skip them
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • Keyboard-operable navigation, menus, and forms, with visible focus states
  • Labeled form fields and error messaging on lead-capture and contact forms

Known limitations

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time fix. Some third-party embeds (such as MLS listing search widgets and map tools) are outside our direct control and may not fully meet WCAG 2.1 AA. We review our own pages on an ongoing basis and prioritize fixes as they're found.

Report an issue

If you encounter a barrier using this Site, please tell us the page URL and a description of the problem so we can fix it. We aim to respond within a few business days.
