Pflugerville FAQ

Quick answers about Pflugerville

Where is Pflugerville in Austin?

Pflugerville is a quieter northeast suburb located between Austin and Round Rock, with easy access to SH-130 and the Domain corridor. The community falls within ZIP code 78660.

What school district serves Pflugerville?

Pflugerville is served by Pflugerville ISD (PISD).

How much do homes cost in Pflugerville?

Pflugerville is one of the more affordable entry points into the Austin metro, drawing strong demand from first-time buyers and investors. The page notes inventory turns reliably and deals move quickly when homes are priced right, but it does not list specific median or range figures; for current numbers you can request the monthly market report.

What is Pflugerville known for?

Pflugerville is known as a family-friendly, affordable, and diverse northeast Austin suburb featuring newer construction, larger lots, and growing amenities. It offers convenient connectivity via SH-130 and the nearby Domain corridor.

What is there to do in Pflugerville?

Pflugerville offers lakes, parks, and family-friendly amenities, with Lake Pflugerville being a popular spot for walking, fishing, kayaking, and paddleboarding. Its location between Austin and Round Rock also puts shopping, dining, and entertainment in the Domain corridor within easy reach.
