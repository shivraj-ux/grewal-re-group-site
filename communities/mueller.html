Mueller FAQ

Quick answers about Mueller

Where is Mueller in Austin?

Mueller is a master-planned, new-urbanism community in central Austin built on the site of the former Robert Mueller Municipal Airport. It sits in the 78723 ZIP code, just northeast of downtown.

What school district serves Mueller?

Mueller is served by Austin Independent School District (Austin ISD).

How much do homes cost in Mueller?

The page does not list specific prices, but describes Mueller as a premium-priced market with predictable inventory cycles. Expect contemporary homes at an above-average central-Austin price point, with strong rental demand that also appeals to investors.

What is Mueller known for?

Mueller is Austin's benchmark new-urbanism community, known for its walkable streets, contemporary mixed-use design, and strong sense of place on the redeveloped former airport site. It is also home to the Dell Seton Medical Center and a popular weekly farmers market.

What is there to do in Mueller?

Mueller offers walkable retail and dining, extensive parks and trails, and the Mueller Farmers Market, along with the Thinkery children's museum. Its central location also puts residents minutes from downtown Austin and the East Austin dining scene.
