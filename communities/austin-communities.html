Shivraj's Take

How to read the Austin market right now

Austin is not one market. It's twenty-plus distinct neighborhoods, each with its own supply rhythm, buyer profile, and pricing logic. West Lake Hills and Pflugerville are in the same metro but they move on different signals. A blanket "Austin is up" or "Austin is down" headline misses what's actually happening on the block you care about.

The monthly view we send is neighborhood-by-neighborhood. We highlight where pricing is firming, where inventory is loosening, and which areas are quietly rewarding patient buyers. If a specific community is on your shortlist, the email tells you what changed there since last month — not just at the metro level.

— Shivraj Grewal, Grewal RE Group
