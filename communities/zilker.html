Zilker FAQ

Quick answers about Zilker

Where is Zilker in Austin?

Zilker is a central-west Austin neighborhood in the 78704 ZIP code, sitting at the intersection of Barton Springs, downtown, and South Lamar. It is one of Austin's most coveted central addresses, walkable to Zilker Park and Barton Springs Pool.

What school district serves Zilker?

Zilker is served by Austin Independent School District (Austin ISD), the district that covers the 78704 area and central Austin.

How much do homes cost in Zilker?

Zilker is a high-end central Austin market (priced in the $$$ tier), where buyers pay a premium for location and lifestyle rather than square footage. Lots tend to be smaller, and the neighborhood's walkability and central-west address keep prices among Austin's most coveted; contact Grewal RE Group for current median sold prices and street-level comps.

What is Zilker known for?

Zilker is known for its walkability to Barton Springs Pool, the Lady Bird Lake Hike-and-Bike Trail, and the South Lamar restaurant corridor, combined with smaller lots and big neighborhood character. It is a central, lifestyle-driven area that is essentially impossible to expand, which keeps it in high demand.

What is there to do in Zilker?

Residents can swim at the spring-fed Barton Springs Pool, walk or bike the trails around Zilker Park and Lady Bird Lake, and dig into the dining and entertainment along the South Lamar corridor. Zilker Park itself hosts festivals and outdoor recreation, all within walking distance of downtown Austin.
