## Quick answers about North Central Austin

### Where is North Central Austin in Austin?

North Central Austin sits along the Burnet Road and Anderson Lane corridor, encompassing established neighborhoods like Allandale, Crestview, Brentwood, and Wooten. It serves as the bridge between downtown Austin and the northern suburbs, covering ZIP codes 78757 and 78758.

### What school district serves North Central Austin?

North Central Austin is served by Austin ISD, the district covering the city's central neighborhoods. The area is noted for its strong central-Austin school options.

### How much do homes cost in North Central Austin?

The area offers solid inventory of mid-century ranches and bungalows priced at a measurable discount to West Austin, making it an affordable entry point into central Austin. It is a mid-market central Austin neighborhood rather than a luxury enclave.

### What is North Central Austin known for?

North Central Austin is known for its established, tree-lined neighborhoods of mid-century ranch homes and bungalows, and for being an affordable, centrally located alternative to pricier West Austin. The area also sees active redevelopment as older homes are renovated or rebuilt.

### What is there to do in North Central Austin?

The Burnet Road and Anderson Lane corridors offer a deep mix of local restaurants, coffee shops, breweries, and shopping just minutes from downtown. Residents also enjoy nearby green space and the central location's quick access to the rest of Austin.
