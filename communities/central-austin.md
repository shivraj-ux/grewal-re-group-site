## Quick answers about Central Austin

### Where is Central Austin in Austin?

Central Austin is the university-adjacent corridor just minutes from downtown and the University of Texas, encompassing neighborhoods like Hyde Park, North Loop, the UT area, and Tarrytown's east edge. The area falls primarily within ZIP codes 78705 and 78751.

### What school district serves Central Austin?

Central Austin is served by Austin ISD, the district covering Hyde Park, North Loop, the UT area, and the surrounding central neighborhoods.

### How much do homes cost in Central Austin?

Central Austin sits in an upper price tier, reflecting its close-in, walkable location near downtown and UT. The page notes that pricing varies meaningfully by exact pocket, so the specific comp set for a given block matters a great deal.

### What is Central Austin known for?

Central Austin is known for its established, tree-lined neighborhoods, historic character homes, mid-density condos, and walkable neighborhood cores. The broad central corridor is prized for its walkability, food scene, Mueller-adjacent location, and strong investor interest.

### What is there to do in Central Austin?

Central Austin offers a vibrant food scene and highly walkable neighborhood cores, with dining and shopping clustered along corridors like North Loop and the Hyde Park area. Its location minutes from downtown and the University of Texas, plus proximity to Mueller, puts parks, restaurants, and campus-area amenities within easy reach.
