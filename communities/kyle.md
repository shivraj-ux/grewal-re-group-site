## Quick answers about Kyle

### Where is Kyle in Austin?

Kyle is a fast-growing southern suburb of Austin located along I-35 in Hays County, roughly 20 miles south of downtown. The community falls in the 78640 ZIP code.

### What school district serves Kyle?

Kyle is served by Hays Consolidated Independent School District (Hays CISD), as noted on the page. The district covers Kyle along with neighboring Buda and parts of southern Hays County.

### How much do homes cost in Kyle?

The page does not list a specific median price, but it describes Kyle as offering new construction inventory at price points well below central Austin. It is one of the area's more affordable, accessible markets, anchored by master-planned communities like Plum Creek and 6 Creeks.

### What is Kyle known for?

Kyle is known as a Hays County growth corridor with strong appreciation, abundant new construction, and increasingly diverse amenities. It is home to master-planned communities including Plum Creek, 6 Creeks, and Bunton Creek.

### What is there to do in Kyle?

Kyle offers the amenity-rich master-planned neighborhoods of Plum Creek, 6 Creeks, and Bunton Creek, with community pools, parks, and trails. Residents also enjoy local shopping and dining along the I-35 corridor and easy access to outdoor recreation throughout Hays County.
