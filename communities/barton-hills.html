Barton Hills FAQ

Quick answers about Barton Hills

Where is Barton Hills in Austin?

Barton Hills is a leafy South Austin neighborhood in the 78704 ZIP code, with direct access to the Barton Creek Greenbelt and Barton Springs and an easy walk to Zilker Park. It sits centrally, roughly a 10-minute commute from downtown Austin.

What school district serves Barton Hills?

Barton Hills is served by Austin Independent School District (Austin ISD), the public district covering this part of South Austin's 78704 area.

How much do homes cost in Barton Hills?

Barton Hills is a quietly luxury, premium-priced neighborhood where mid-century cottages sit alongside contemporary rebuilds. Homes tend to be smaller with modest lot sizes, but as the page notes, the lifestyle premium for the central location and Greenbelt access is real.

What is Barton Hills known for?

Barton Hills is known for its South Austin character and direct Greenbelt access, blending walkable, central living with a mix of mid-century cottages and modern rebuilds. It is prized for the lifestyle of Greenbelt walks, food culture, and a quick downtown commute.

What is there to do in Barton Hills?

Residents enjoy direct access to the Barton Creek Greenbelt for hiking and trails, plus swimming at Barton Springs Pool and the green space and events at nearby Zilker Park. The walkable, central location also puts South Austin's well-known food and dining culture within easy reach.
