Dripping Springs FAQ

Quick answers about Dripping Springs

Where is Dripping Springs in Austin?

Dripping Springs sits in the Texas Hill Country west of Austin, known as the 'Gateway to the Hill Country.' It falls in the 78620 ZIP code and is roughly a 30-minute commute from Austin.

What school district serves Dripping Springs?

Dripping Springs is served by Dripping Springs ISD (DSISD), which the page highlights as one of the area's strong school districts.

How much do homes cost in Dripping Springs?

The page doesn't list specific prices, but it positions Dripping Springs as a market of acreage, sprawling lots, and master-planned luxury communities, with a priceRange noted as $$$. Expect higher-end, land-oriented pricing where buyers trade commute time for more elbow room.

What is Dripping Springs known for?

Dripping Springs is known as the 'Gateway to the Hill Country,' famous for its wineries, distilleries, and sprawling acreage west of Austin. It also offers master-planned communities like Belterra, Caliterra, and Headwaters alongside strong DSISD schools.

What is there to do in Dripping Springs?

Dripping Springs is a hub for Hill Country wineries and distilleries, with bigger lots and outdoor lifestyle appeal. The surrounding area is well known for swimming holes and natural attractions such as Hamilton Pool Preserve, along with local dining, tasting rooms, and access to nearby Lake Travis.
