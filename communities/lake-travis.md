Lake Travis FAQ

## Quick answers about Lake Travis

### Where is Lake Travis in Austin?

Lake Travis is west of Austin along the lake's shoreline in the Texas Hill Country, spanning sub-areas such as Steiner Ranch, Lakeway, Bee Cave, The Hollows, Hudson Bend, Point Venture, and Lago Vista. It covers ZIP codes 78732, 78734, and 78738.

### What school district serves Lake Travis?

Lake Travis is served by the top-rated Lake Travis ISD.

### How much do homes cost in Lake Travis?

Lake Travis is a luxury market made up of three distinct tiers, a waterfront premium, a lake-access premium, and a pure-view premium, with pricing that varies by sub-area such as Hudson Bend, Point Venture, and Lago Vista. The area offers a meaningful price-per-square-foot advantage over the 78746 (Westlake) ZIP code.

### What is Lake Travis known for?

Lake Travis is known for waterfront and Hill Country living, with lakefront estates, lake-access neighborhoods, and view homes. The combination of direct lake access and highly rated Lake Travis ISD schools is a key draw.

### What is there to do in Lake Travis?

Life on Lake Travis centers on the water, with boating, swimming, and lakeside recreation throughout the Hill Country shoreline. The surrounding communities of Lakeway, Bee Cave, and Steiner Ranch add golf, dining, shopping, and scenic trails.
