Travis Heights FAQ

Quick answers about Travis Heights

Where is Travis Heights in Austin?

Travis Heights is a leafy residential neighborhood in South Austin, sitting on the quiet residential side of South Congress (SoCo). It falls within the 78704 ZIP code, just a short walk from South Congress's shops and restaurants and minutes from Lady Bird Lake and downtown across the bridge.

What school district serves Travis Heights?

Travis Heights is served by Austin Independent School District (Austin ISD), the district covering the 78704 area of central and south Austin.

How much do homes cost in Travis Heights?

The page does not list a specific median or price range, but it describes Travis Heights as a highly desirable South Austin pocket with tight inventory and fierce buyer competition, and marks it as an upper-tier ($$$) market. Pricing reflects its eclectic, walkable character and proximity to South Congress, so reach out for a current street-level comp analysis.

What is Travis Heights known for?

Travis Heights is known as one of the most charming pockets of South Austin, with tree-lined streets and eclectic mid-century homes. It is prized for its walkability to South Congress, the Continental Club, and downtown just over the bridge.

What is there to do in Travis Heights?

Travis Heights residents are a five-minute walk from South Congress's restaurants, shops, and live music at the Continental Club. The neighborhood is also close to Lady Bird Lake, its hike-and-bike trails, and downtown Austin just across the bridge.
