## Quick answers about Downtown Austin

### Where is Downtown Austin in Austin?

Downtown Austin is the urban core of the city, covering districts like Rainey Street, the 2nd Street District, Seaholm, and the Northshore. It sits in ZIP code 78701, walkable to Lady Bird Lake, the Capitol corridor, and the entertainment district.

### What school district serves Downtown Austin?

Downtown Austin (78701) is served by Austin Independent School District (Austin ISD).

### How much do homes cost in Downtown Austin?

Downtown Austin is a condo market, where verticality buys skyline views and walkability. It sits in a higher price tier (the page rates it $$$), best for buyers who value lock-and-leave living over backyard space.

### What is Downtown Austin known for?

Downtown Austin is known for high-rise condo living in the urban core, with distinct districts including Rainey Street, the 2nd Street District, Seaholm, and the Northshore. It offers skyline views and walkability to nearly everything in the city center.

### What is there to do in Downtown Austin?

Downtown Austin puts you within walking distance of Lady Bird Lake and its hike-and-bike trail, the Capitol corridor, and the entertainment district. Rainey Street and the 2nd Street District add bars, restaurants, and nightlife right in the urban core.
