# Lakeway

Lakeside Hill Country living on Lake Travis, just ~25 miles west of downtown Austin. Top-rated Lake Travis ISD, resort golf, marinas, and boating define one of the metro's most desirable lake communities.

Lakeway at a Glance

## What buyers should know about Lakeway, TX

**Median home price:** Most non-waterfront homes trade from the high $600Ks to around $1M, while golf-course and Lake Travis waterfront estates commonly range from $2M to $8M+. Pricing is driven heavily by water access, view, and which sub-community (Rough Hollow, Flintrock Falls, The Hills, Old Lakeway, Serene Hills) you target.

**Schools:** Lakeway is served by the highly rated **Lake Travis ISD**, anchored by Lake Travis High School and Hudson Bend / Lakeway elementary and middle campuses, a primary draw for relocating families.

**Commute to downtown Austin:** Roughly 30–40 minutes via RM 620 and Loop 360, or Highway 71 to MoPac. Closer to the Galleria, Hill Country Galleria, and the growing 620/71 employment and retail corridors.

**Neighborhood character:** Resort-style Hill Country living, golf at Lakeway Resort, Flintrock Falls, and Yaupon; marinas and boat slips on Lake Travis; the World of Tennis; trails, parks, and a true small-city feel within the Austin metro.

## What's actually happening in Lakeway right now

Lakeway buyers are really buying a lifestyle, lake access, golf, and Lake Travis ISD, so the smart move is to anchor on water access and view first, then the house. ## Quick answers about Lakeway

### Where is Lakeway in Austin?

Lakeway sits on Lake Travis in the Hill Country, roughly 25 miles west of downtown Austin. The page lists ZIP codes 78734 and 78738, with a commute to downtown of about 30 to 40 minutes via RM 620 and Loop 360 or Highway 71 to MoPac.

### What school district serves Lakeway?

Lakeway is served by the highly rated Lake Travis ISD, anchored by Lake Travis High School along with Hudson Bend and Lakeway elementary and middle campuses. Strong schools are a primary draw for relocating families.

### How much do homes cost in Lakeway?

According to the page, most non-waterfront homes trade from the high $600Ks to around $1M, while golf-course and Lake Travis waterfront estates commonly range from $2M to $8M+. Pricing depends heavily on water access, view, and which sub-community you target, such as Rough Hollow, Flintrock Falls, The Hills, Old Lakeway, or Serene Hills.

### What is Lakeway known for?

Lakeway is known for resort-style Hill Country living on Lake Travis, with golf at Lakeway Resort, Flintrock Falls, and Yaupon, plus marinas and boat slips. It also offers the World of Tennis, trails, and parks, with a true small-city feel within the Austin metro.

### What is there to do in Lakeway?

Lakeway centers on Lake Travis boating and lake access, multiple golf courses including Lakeway Resort, Flintrock Falls, and Yaupon, and the World of Tennis facility. Residents also enjoy marinas, trails, and parks, with nearby shopping at the Hill Country Galleria along the 620/71 corridor.
