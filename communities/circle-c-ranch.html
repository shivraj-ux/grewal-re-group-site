Circle C Ranch FAQ

Quick answers about Circle C Ranch

Where is Circle C Ranch in Austin?

Circle C Ranch is a master-planned community in Southwest Austin, Texas. It sits within the 78739 ZIP code.

What school district serves Circle C Ranch?

Circle C Ranch is served by Austin ISD (AISD), and the community is known for its top-rated AISD schools.

How much do homes cost in Circle C Ranch?

Circle C Ranch offers a wide spread of price points rather than a single luxury tier, with predictable inventory across the community. As Shivraj notes, it carries less of a luxury price tag and more lifestyle value, making it relatively accessible for a master-planned Austin neighborhood.

What is Circle C Ranch known for?

Circle C Ranch is known as a family-friendly, master-planned community in Southwest Austin with strong amenities, including pools, golf, parks, and trails. It is best for buyers who prioritize schools, amenities, and community lifestyle over downtown adjacency.

What is there to do in Circle C Ranch?

Residents enjoy community pools, walking and biking trails, parks, and golf within the neighborhood. The area also offers easy access to nearby Southwest Austin greenbelts and outdoor recreation, reinforcing its family-focused, amenity-rich appeal.
