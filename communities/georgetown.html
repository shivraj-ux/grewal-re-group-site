Georgetown FAQ

Quick answers about Georgetown

Where is Georgetown in Austin?

Georgetown is the historic county seat located about 30 minutes north of Austin along the north corridor. It spans ZIP codes 78626, 78628, and 78633, with Hill Country topography on its west side.

What school district serves Georgetown?

Georgetown is served by Georgetown ISD (GISD), which the community page highlights for its strong schools.

How much do homes cost in Georgetown?

Georgetown is a family-friendly market with strong long-term appreciation, generally sitting in a mid-to-upper price tier. The page does not list specific median or range figures, so reach out to Grewal RE Group for the latest Georgetown pricing data.

What is Georgetown known for?

Georgetown is known for its iconic historic courthouse square, the San Gabriel River, Southwestern University, and the Sun City active-adult retirement community. It is the anchor city of the north corridor north of Austin.

What is there to do in Georgetown?

Georgetown offers Lake Georgetown for boating and recreation, the San Gabriel River and its trails and parks, and a vibrant historic downtown square with dining and shops. The Sun City community adds golf and amenities for active-adult residents.
