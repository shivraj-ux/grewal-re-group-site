Buda FAQ

Quick answers about Buda

Where is Buda in Austin?

Buda sits just south of Austin along I-35 in Hays County, carrying the ZIP code 78610. It is a practical option for buyers who work in south or central Austin and want an easy commute with more space.

What school district serves Buda?

Buda is served by Hays Consolidated Independent School District (Hays CISD).

How much do homes cost in Buda?

Buda is positioned as a value-oriented market where buyers get more home for the money compared with central Austin. The page emphasizes affordability and space over central proximity rather than a specific price figure, with a mix of established neighborhoods and newer master-planned communities.

What is Buda known for?

Buda is known as a fast-growing south Austin suburb that blends established neighborhoods with new master-planned communities. Buyers are drawn to its value pricing, growing infrastructure, good schools, and accessible commutes.

What is there to do in Buda?

Buda offers a walkable historic Main Street with local shops and dining, plus community parks and the popular Buda Mill & Grain District. Its location on I-35 also puts shopping, the annual Buda Wiener Dog Races, and nearby Kyle and south Austin attractions within easy reach.
