Davenport Ranch FAQ

Quick answers about Davenport Ranch

Where is Davenport Ranch in Austin?

It sits in West Austin, ZIP 78746, along Loop 360 between downtown and Lake Austin, just north of Westlake. Most of the neighborhood is about 10 to 15 minutes from downtown.

What school district serves Davenport Ranch?

Eanes ISD, one of the highest-rated public districts in Texas: Bridge Point Elementary, Hill Country Middle School, and Westlake High School. The Eanes zoning is a primary driver of home values here.

How much do homes cost in Davenport Ranch?

The 78746 ZIP has a typical home value near $1.72M (Zillow via KXAN, April 2026). Within Davenport Ranch, prices generally run from about $900K for patio homes and condos to $5M and up for golf-course and Lake Austin estates.

Is Davenport Ranch a gated community?

Partly. It has several gated estate sections alongside open residential streets. The most exclusive pockets sit around the Pete Dye golf course at Austin Country Club and along the Lake Austin frontage.

What is there to do in Davenport Ranch?

Golf at Austin Country Club, Lake Austin access, and shopping and dining at Davenport Village on Loop 360. The 360 corridor also puts the Pennybacker Bridge, hike-and-bike trails, and downtown within a short drive.
