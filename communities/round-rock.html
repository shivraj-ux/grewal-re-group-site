Round Rock FAQ

Quick answers about Round Rock

Where is Round Rock in Austin?

Round Rock is Austin's largest northern suburb, located in the North Austin tech corridor. It spans ZIP codes 78664, 78665, and 78681.

What school district serves Round Rock?

Round Rock is served by Round Rock ISD, one of the area's well-regarded school districts and a major draw for families relocating to the suburb.

How much do homes cost in Round Rock?

The page does not list a specific median price, but it positions Round Rock as a relatively affordable, mid-market suburban option whose affordability and commute access continue to draw buyers priced out of central Austin.

What is Round Rock known for?

Round Rock is known for its strong corporate base anchored by Dell's headquarters corridor, established family-friendly neighborhoods, and top Round Rock ISD schools. Its mix of tech-corridor jobs and suburban amenities keeps the buyer pool wide.

What is there to do in Round Rock?

Round Rock offers suburban amenities including Old Settlers Park, the Dell Diamond minor-league ballpark, and the Round Rock Premium Outlets, plus parks, trails, and dining. The historic downtown and nearby Brushy Creek trail system add to the area's recreation options.
