Everything we hear most often, answered without the fluff.

Shivraj combines deep local knowledge across both West Austin luxury markets and Central/East Austin growth corridors. With 100+ transactions, $100M+ in career volume, CLHMS Guild and CNE designations, and 149 five-star reviews, he brings data-driven strategy and honest communication to every client. Brand promise: People First, Straight Answers, Strong Results.
Look for verified transaction history in your target neighborhoods, a clear marketing strategy for luxury properties, and a reputation for honest communication. Shivraj has closed 100+ transactions totaling over $100M in volume, focus areas include Westlake Hills, Barton Creek, and Davenport Ranch.
Westlake Hills (78746) is known for Eanes ISD, one of the top-rated school districts in Texas. Homes typically range from $1.2M to $8M+. Mature tree canopy, low density, proximity to Barton Creek Greenbelt, and a 10 to 15 minute commute to downtown Austin.
Both are premier West Austin luxury areas in the top-rated Eanes ISD, but they feel different. Westlake Hills (78746) is an established residential community 10–15 minutes from downtown, with mature tree canopy, smaller lots, and homes from $1.2M to $8M+. Barton Creek (78735 / 78746) is more spread out and amenity-driven — gated golf communities anchored by the Barton Creek Resort, one-to-several-acre estate lots, a country-club lifestyle, and more new luxury construction, with a slightly longer 15–25 minute downtown commute. Westlake offers proximity and a traditional neighborhood feel; Barton Creek offers acreage and resort amenities.
Buyers: discovery call → financing coordination → neighborhood comparison → tailored search → offer strategy with comp data → inspection guidance → closing management. Sellers: walk-through consult → data-driven pricing → preparation plan → marketing launch → showing management → negotiation → contract-to-close coordination.
Start by identifying priorities: commute, schools, lifestyle, budget. We provide neighborhood shortlists, virtual and in-person tours, side-by-side comparisons of Austin areas vs. your current city, and coordination with your current-city agent. Austin spans four counties (Travis, Hays, Williamson, Bastrop) with different tax rates, school districts, and commute patterns.
As of June 2026, the Austin metro had roughly 12,508 active listings, a $440,000 median sold price, and about 4.7 months of inventory (Unlock MLS / Austin Board of REALTORS®). The market is balanced-to-buyer-favorable at the entry level; luxury ($1M+) remains supply-constrained. Conditions vary sharply by neighborhood — contact us for a neighborhood-specific report.
Austin's luxury segment ($1M+) is outperforming the broader market. The Institute for Luxury Home Marketing June 2026 report shows: $1,945,000 median sold price, 25 days on market, +10.3% sales volume YoY, and -5.8% inventory YoY — fewer homes, faster sales. Demand is strongest in Westlake Hills, Rollingwood, and Barton Creek.
New construction offers modern floor plans, builder warranties, and energy efficiency. Resale homes in Westlake Hills, Tarrytown, and Barton Creek offer mature landscaping, larger lots, and proximity to central Austin. We advise on both and can represent you with builders to negotiate upgrades, pricing, and contract terms.
Luxury homes ($1.5M+) require a phased strategy: private exclusive outreach to qualified buyers → coming-soon period → full market launch with professional photography, targeted digital marketing, and agent-to-agent outreach. Expect 60 to 120 days from preparation to closing. Pricing accuracy is critical in the luxury segment.
