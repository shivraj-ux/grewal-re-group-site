Frequently asked questions

How can I find out what my Austin home is worth?
The most accurate way is a comparative market analysis (CMA), a review of recent sales of similar homes in your neighborhood adjusted for your home's size, condition, and features. Request one through this tool and Grewal RE Group will prepare it.
Are online home value estimates accurate?
Automated estimates can be off by tens of thousands of dollars, especially in non-disclosure states like Texas where sale prices are not public record. A local agent's comp-based valuation is far more reliable.
How much does a home valuation cost?
Nothing. A professional comparative market analysis from Grewal RE Group is free and comes with no obligation to list.
How long does it take to get my home valuation?
After you submit your home's details, Grewal RE Group typically returns a personalized valuation within one to two business days.
